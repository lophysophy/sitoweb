Hello!

I am Sofia Lucarelli, a young artist working in visual development, concept art, character design and 2D animation.

I am currently studying for my Master Degree in Character Animation at Gobelins in Paris (CRFA, class of 2027). My work moves between digital and traditional media, I like to incorporate traditional techniques into my projects and sometimes challenge myself to achieve that look only through digital. I love painting and playing with colors!
