Hi!

I am Sofia Lucarelli, lophy.sophy on Instagram. I'm a creative, dynamic, and proactive young artist. I am passionate about investing my full dedication into everything I do. I love caring for every project down to its details, to ensure it resonates with its intended audience. My goal is to convey emotions through drawing, touching the hearts of those around me.

My work moves between digital and traditional media, I like to incorporate traditional techniques into my projects and sometimes challenge myself to achieve that look only through digital. I love painting and playing with colors!

Currently I am studying for my Master Degree in Character Animation and Animated Filmmaking at Gobelins Paris (CRFA, class of 2027). Before that I earned a BA in Illustration and Animation at IED Rome.

Want to work together? Write me at lophy.sophy@gmail.com.
